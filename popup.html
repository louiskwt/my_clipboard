Smart Clipboard


A Chrome Extension designed for CS50x Final Project

Created by Ka Wing (Louis) Tsang


Simpy copy any text you want, and then open the clipboard by hovering over to your top right hand corner.

It requires you to give permission for the browser to allow access to the information in your clipboard

In side the clipboard, you can find the text you just copied stored in a yellow card.

The clipboard can store up to 4 cards, and you can click the card to copy the text inside the card.
